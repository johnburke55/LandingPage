Intro

Small businesses and brands tend to cast the troubles of big data to the side. Look, I get it, you have way bigger things on your plate. You're working with shippers and sellers and buyers and employees and taxes, and data comes along as just another bite for you to chew.

Johnny Burke data is designed for small business. We're here to show you what your customers' data can do for your bottom line, and how to take your stake of the world's most valuable resource: data. We can drive down the road of affinity and loyalty, explore your options in social media targeting, and give you a 360 degree view of your customer. See what they want, when they want it, and how they want to know about it. Customers are what you appreciate the most, so let's do what we can to keep them in love with your brand.

About Burke Data

Burke Data is a full-service data analytics and digital marketing firm located in Nashville, Tennessee. We have teams and locations throughout Kentucky, Ohio, and Tennessee. Our team began in 2014 with the establishment of Johnny Burke's first digital marketing firm when he was a sophomore at Western Kentucky University. From there the entrepreneurial spirit has lived on as he entered the corporate workforce with The Balanced Man and now Burke Data.

Burke Data wants to continue to serve Kentucky, Tennessee, and Ohio's small businesses with the data platform they deserve. The men and women of small business in the United States work hard to make their own success, and studying the fine details of digital marketing and data should not overrun their love for their business. Let us take that load off of your hands, and help carry your brand into the future of American business.
